Collections

This site is built with the static site generator Jekyll and the vast majority of content here is organized using Jekyll's native collections functionality. Collections include both posts and pages, all of which are written in Markdown and then rendered in HTML by Jekyll. The permalink for my collections is /collections.

H-Card

This is my h-card. As per the Microformats Wiki, “h-card is a simple, open format for publishing people and organisations on the web.” All you need to do to create your own h-card is add a few html tags to your website. You can validate that your h-card is working correctly at IndieWebify.me. The permalink for my h-card is /h-card.

Button Garden

88x31 buttons were a staple of the early, independent internet, often seen on GeoCities sites. Buttons are a fun way to link to other sites you like, or flag important details about the site or the site owner. You can save buttons I use below to use on your own site, or click through to find thousands more at the linked pages: IndieWeb, The Cyber Vanguard, The Yesterweb, Capstasher, A. N. Lucas, Deadnet, 88x31.nl, Hellnet. Or, you can make your own with Sadgrl or Hekate’s button makers. The permalink for my button garden is /buttons.
