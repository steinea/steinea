This is the human-readable sitemap of my root-level pages. My not-so-human-readable sitemap.xml can be found at /sitemap.xml, and is generated by the jekyll-sitemap plugin. As per sitemaps.org, a sitemap is an “easy way for webmasters to inform search engines about pages on their sites that are available for crawling.” Importantly, given the rapid proliferation of AI scrapers, and as recommended by Jay X Peet on Twitter, I have also added a robots.txt, found at /robots.txt (which I copied from Jay's at the Game UI Database), to disallow this type of web crawling of my site.
